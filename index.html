Our Story

From Passion to Plate

A Legacy of Flavor

At just 22, I’m an economics graduate with 4 years of retail management experience — but my true passion lies in reimagining desserts. I believe indulgence shouldn’t come at the cost of health.

That’s why I handcraft each dessert using only the finest natural ingredients — no preservatives, no chemicals, and absolutely no guilt.

Every recipe is made with love, using millet flour, whole wheat flour, and natural sweeteners like honey, dates, and jaggery.

You won’t find any refined sugar, refined flour, eggs, or palm oil here — just clean, wholesome treats that nourish your body and satisfy your soul.
